\section*{Supplementary Material:\normalfont{Towards Ocean Model-Driven Robotic Exploration}}
\label{sec:annex}

This Supplementary Material provides additional analyses and figures
that support the main manuscript while keeping the core text
concise. In particular, it includes supplementary visualizations used
to (i) clarify operational and methodological aspects of the
\emph{sample-assimilate-predict-direct} loop, and (ii) enable direct,
side-by-side comparisons between model solutions and in situ
observations for readers who wish to examine those aspects in greater
detail.


Fig. \ref{fig:real_planned} provides a high-resolution comparison
between planned and realised AUV trajectories for the relevant
deployment days. For quantitative context, the maximum deviation
between the planned and realised paths (from top-left to bottom-right
panels) is 256~m, 318~m, 218~m, 155~m, and 279~m, respectively.

 \begin{figure}[!b]
   \centering
   \includegraphics[scale=0.33]{fig/real_vs_planned.pdf}
   \caption{\rtwo{Planned versus realized AUV trajectories plotted on
       the corresponding daily model-derived uncertainty maps. Panels
       correspond to XP2 (29\textsuperscript{th} Oct), XP5
       (29\textsuperscript{th}--30\textsuperscript{th} Oct;
       30\textsuperscript{th}--31\textsuperscript{st} Oct), and XP3
       (30\textsuperscript{th}--31\textsuperscript{st} Oct;
       31\textsuperscript{st} Oct). The planned route is shown as
       markers and a black line, and the realized trajectory as a
       green line.}}
   \label{fig:real_planned}
 \end{figure}

 In our algorithmic framework, routes are not designed to target a
 single location of maximum pointwise uncertainty; instead, a planned
 route is selected to maximize the \textit{accumulated}
 path-integrated uncertainty that can be sampled under the relevant
 operational constraints. Fig. \ref{fig:cumulative} complements that
 perspective by summarising how the temperature error metric
 accumulates over mission duration across the deployments, providing a
 compact diagnostic for comparing error growth behavior among
 scenarios.
 
 \begin{figure}[!t]
   \centering 
   \includegraphics[scale=0.33]{fig/cumulative_error.pdf}
   \caption{\rone{Cumulative temperature error as a function of
       mission duration for the different AUV missions shown in the
       legend. Each colored curve corresponds to a mission after
       generating a new plan, allowing a direct comparison of how
       \emph{uncertainty} accumulates over time across missions.}}
   \label{fig:cumulative}
 \end{figure}
