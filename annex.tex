\section*{Supplementary Material:\normalfont{'Towards Ocean Model-Driven Robotic Exploration'}}
\label{sec:annex}

This Supplementary Material provides additional analyses and figures that support the main manuscript while keeping the core text concise. In particular, it includes supplementary visualizations used to (i) clarify operational and methodological aspects of the sample--assimilate--predict--direct loop, and (ii) enable direct, side-by-side comparisons between model solutions and in situ observations for readers who wish to examine those aspects in greater detail.


Figure \ref{fig:real_planned} provides a high-resolution comparison between planned and realised AUV trajectories for the relevant deployment days. For quantitative context, the maximum deviation between the planned and realised paths (from top-left to bottom-right panels) is 256~m, 318~m, 218~m, 155~m, and 279~m, respectively.

 \begin{figure}[!b]
      \centering
      \includegraphics[scale=0.33]{fig/real_vs_planned.pdf}
      \caption{\rtwo{Planned versus realized AUV trajectories plotted on the
        corresponding daily model-derived uncertainty maps. Panels
        correspond to XP2 (29 Oct), XP5 (29--30 Oct; 30--31 Oct), and
        XP3 (30--31 Oct; 31 Oct). The planned route is shown as
        markers and a black line, and the realized trajectory as a
        green line.}}
      \label{fig:real_planned}
    \end{figure}

In our guidance framework, routes are not designed to target a single location of maximum pointwise uncertainty; instead, the planned route is selected to maximize the \textit{accumulated} path-integrated uncertainty that can be sampled under the relevant operational constraints. Figure \ref{fig:cumulative} complements that perspective by summarising how the temperature error metric accumulates over mission duration across the deployments/experiments, providing a compact diagnostic for comparing error growth behavior among scenarios. 

     \begin{figure}[!t]
       \centering
       \includegraphics[scale=0.33]{fig/cumulative_error.pdf}
       \caption{\rone{Cumulative temperature error as a function of mission
         duration for the different AUV missions shown in the
         legend. Each colored curve corresponds to a mission after
         generating a new plan, allowing a direct comparison of how
         \textit{uncertainty collected} accumulates over time across
         missions.}}
  \label{fig:cumulative}
\end{figure}
